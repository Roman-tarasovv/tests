How it works

1. Connect
Link your TRON wallet via a secure, read‑only interface.
2. Analyze
We analyze on‑chain history, counterparties and protocol interactions; we cross‑check sanctions/risk lists.
3. Report
You receive a simple report with a clear risk label and key drivers.

Features

Sanctions screening
Checks against sanctioned addresses and services.
Source of funds
Estimates the origin of funds across incoming flows and bridges.
Counterparty risk
Identifies interactions with exchanges, DeFi protocols and mixers.

Download

Privacy

FAQ

Do I have to sign?
Connection may request a safe confirmation. We do not move funds or charge fees without approval.
What data do you use?
Public transfers, contract interactions, aggregated risk sources and open sanctions lists.
Is this financial advice?
No. The report is informational and supports personal risk awareness.
Multiple addresses?
Yes. After connecting, choose which address to analyze.
